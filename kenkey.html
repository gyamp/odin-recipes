THE FIRE OF THE THROAT MONSTER| PREPARATION

    Ingredients

  • Cassava Flour
  • Water
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Pepper
  • Salt
  • Meat
  • Dried Fish

    Preparation

    1. Boil water in a pot
    2. Gradually add cassava flour while stirring continuously.
    3. Continue stirring until the mixture thickens and becomes smooth.
    4. In a separate pot, heat oil and sauté onions, tomatoes, pepper and salt [per your taste] for the light soup.
    5. Add a piece of meat and desired quantity of dried fish to the soup and cook until tender.
    6. Serve the kokonte with the light soup spread all over in the bowl served.
