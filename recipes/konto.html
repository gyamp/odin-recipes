HOW FUFU AND LIGHT SOUP IS PREPARED

    Ingredients

  • cassava
  • Plantain
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Pepper
  • Salt
  • Meat
  • Dried Fish

    Preparation

  1. Peel the cassava and plantain, cut into chunks, and boil until soft.
  2. Mash the boiled cassava and plantain together with a pestle until smooth to make fufu.
  3. In a separate pot, heat oil and sauté onions, tomatoes,pepper and salt [per your taste] for the light soup.
  4. Add a piece of meat and desired quantity of dried fish to the soup and cook until tender.
  5. Serve the fufu with the light soup spread all over in the bowl served.
