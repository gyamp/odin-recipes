RICE & BEANS MONSTER | PREPARATION

    Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Beans
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Pepper
  • Salt
  • Meat
  • Dried Fish

    Preparation Process

    1. Rinse the rice and beans thoroughly.
    2. Cook the rice and beans together in a pot with water until tender.
    3. In a separate pot, heat oil and sauté onions, tomatoes, lots of pepper and salt [per your taste] for the tomato Stew.
    4. Add a piece of meat and desired quantity of fish, wele to the stew and cook until tender.
    5. Serve the waakye with the tomato stew spread all over in the bowl served.
