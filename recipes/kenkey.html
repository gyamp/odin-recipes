KENKEY | THE FIRE OF THE THROAT MONSTER

THE FIRE OF THE THROAT MONSTER | PREPARATION

    Ingredients

  • Cassava Flour
  • maize Flour
  • Water
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Red And Green Pepper
  • Salt
  • Meat
  • Dried Maize husk
  • Fresh Fish
  • Coconut Oil
  • Ghanaian Pear
  • Crab, shrimps

    Preparation

    1. Mash your cassava and maize flour in water in a pot(20;50:25 proportion)
    2. allow to settle in the mixing pot and ferment for up between 48 hours to 72 hours
    3. After fermentation, wrap portions into balls inside dried maize husk per the size of the ball you prefer.
    4. Boil water in a pot and place the kenkey balls in the boiling water.
    5. Gradually add cassava flour while stirring continuously.
    6. Continue stirring until the mixture thickens and becomes smooth.
    7. In a separate pot, heat oil and sauté onions, to fry your meat, fish, crab and or shrimps
    8. Grind your tomatoes, pepper and salt [per your taste] in locally made or electric grinder.
    9. Serve the kenkey with the saurce and protiens on the side of the bowl served.
